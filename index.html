Seasons of Giving

Why We Need This

The gift cards we sell at the hub are boring; face it some random pattern and a logo is just terrible. That's why I propose we have special designs for the seasons. I've done at least 48 hours of lab time at the hub and I've only seen 1 person buy gift cards. With these designs, the gift cards are more lively making them perfect for gifting. I've seen time and time again students begging friends to buy them stuff at the hub, so giving them a gift card for a special occasion would be perfect. Most teachers at this school also enjoy the coffee made by the hub, so giving them a fun lively gift card would be also perfect for thanking them.

Buying Options

Normal Gift Card

A standard barcode gift card same as we currently use just with my designs. From Duracard

Cost: $2.10/card for 100 cards with magnetic stripes and variable data.

Pros:

Same system as we currently use

Cost efficent

Cons:

100 cards might be too many for each

Visa Gift Cards

Selling a Visa gift card with our custom design on it. This will be sold as a product rather than a normal Hub gift card.

Cost: Variable

Pros:

Instant Profit

Customer can spend the money whereever they would like.

Cons:

Either we have to front the cost of the money on the card or work with Visa to work out a way where we are able to activate them.

DIY

Glue/tap the design on top of curent design and then laminate hopefully not with tape like me.

Cost: FREE

Pros:

Free

Don't have to buy new cards

Cons:

Low qualty

Time consuming to make
